## Specialized Equipment and Process

- Roller blinds are cleaned on dedicated blind-cleaning workstations with special detergents chosen for the fabric structure.

- Before packaging, carpets are processed with dusting machines to reduce dust and loose residue.
- After cleaning, each carpet is carefully packed as part of a controlled delivery-preparation workflow.

## Wet Clean Approach

- Wet clean is a computer-controlled professional textile care process, not the same as regular home laundry.
- It works with water and biodegradable cleaning products.
- Washing, drying and finishing steps are adjusted according to fabric type.
- It is suitable for wool, silk, cashmere and other delicate garments when the right program is used.

## Difference From Solvent Systems

- It offers a water-based approach instead of relying on PERC and similar solvents.
- It aims for a fresher result without leaving solvent odor on garments.
- It supports an environmentally conscious process that does not generate hazardous chemical waste.
- It is designed to feel gentler for delicate garments and sensitive users.

## FAQ

- Wet clean is not the same as standard laundry; it is a more specialized and fabric-sensitive process.
- Stain removal is not guaranteed on every item; stain type, fabric structure and waiting time all affect the result.
- Greenlux first evaluates the fabric and stain, then applies the most suitable pre-treatment process.
